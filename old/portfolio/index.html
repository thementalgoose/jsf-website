Am I Wrong
Hack24 2017

Last year, Sebastian Ghetu, Jono Alford (founder of Roboto Studio and I were lucky enough to attend the Hack24 event in the heart of Nottingham. Together we built Am I Wrong, a moral ambiguity application which provides a platform to ask your risk-ay questions and get community feedback, and we got Second place! :) With this awesome video

This year we're going back with the same goal: To make something cool! Ping me a message if you have an idea of what you would like to see us do!
